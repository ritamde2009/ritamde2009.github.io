The Rectangle on line one creates the third quadrant of the visual, and the lines in line 2-3 are the outlines for each quadrant. The label creates the title of the quadrant, and the ovals create the clouds with a gradient. Line 7 creates the green stem, and the 2 polygons make the leaf outlines. Line 9 & 11 create the black lines on the leaves, and the last line creates the brown ground.
In this project, I used the code that I learned in class for shapes, colors, borders, gradients, and more to create a visual that shares 4 of 17 global goals of the UN.
