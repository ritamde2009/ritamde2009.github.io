The rectangle and lines at the beginning are part of the background, with the green rectangle creating the bottom-left quadrant of the design, and the lines creating the outline for each quadrant. The label creates the text at the top. The oval lines create clouds, and the polygon lines create the shape of the leaves. The line created on line 7 creates the stem, and the last 2 lines create lines making the black lines across the leaves.
In this project, I used the code that I learned in class for shapes, colors, borders, gradients, and more to create a visual that shares 4 of 17 global goals of the UN.
